As the founder of Podverse, I wanted to take a moment to share the inspiration behind our platform and our vision for the future.
It all started when I realized how difficult it was to discover new podcasts that aligned with my interests. As someone who loves learning and exploring new topics, I found myself frustrated by the limitations of current podcast discovery tools. Many platforms rely on algorithms that prioritize popular shows, leaving lesser-known gems hidden from view. I knew there had to be a better way. That’s when I set out to create Podverse, a platform that puts discovery front and center. Our goal is to create a community of passionate podcast listeners who can easily find and share their favorite shows.
